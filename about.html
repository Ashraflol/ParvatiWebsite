About Parvati Engineers

Building Trust Through Engineering Excellence Since 2000

Our Legacy

Established in 2000, Parvati Engineers has evolved from a supplier of critical preparatory components to a comprehensive solution provider in the sugar industry. With over two decades of hands-on experience, we deliver reliable engineering solutions backed by proven expertise.

Our journey began with supplying cane cutting knives, hammers, and polymer runners. Today, we are recognized leaders in turnkey project execution and plant modernization, serving customers across India and internationally.

Our Expertise

We specialize in preparatory device assemblies, Mill House and Boiling House equipment, and complete turnkey project execution. Since 2023, we've pioneered economical solutions by intelligently combining existing machinery with new, high-performance equipment.

Our focus on performance, efficiency, and cost optimization ensures we deliver tailor-made solutions that provide maximum value while ensuring long equipment life and dependable operation.

Our Journey

2000 - Foundation

Parvati Engineers was established with a vision to provide quality engineering components to the sugar industry. We started as suppliers of critical preparatory components including cane cutting knives, hammers, and polymer runners, building strong relationships with sugar mills across Maharashtra.

2010 - Expansion & Growth

Expanded our capabilities significantly to include preparatory device assemblies and Mill House equipment manufacturing. Our reputation for quality and reliability helped us secure larger contracts and expand our customer base throughout India.

2020 - Turnkey Solutions

Established ourselves as comprehensive solution providers with multiple successful turnkey projects. Completed major installations including the 2500 TCD Rajapur Sugar project and the international 1250 TCD Bijiya Shree Sugar Mills project in Nepal.

2023 - Present Innovation

Pioneered economical turnkey solutions by combining refurbished and new machinery, setting new industry standards. This innovative approach has made us the preferred choice for plant modernization, capacity expansion, and cost-sensitive projects.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of innovative, sustainable, and economical sugar plant solutions in India and beyond. We aim to set industry benchmarks in quality, performance, and customer satisfaction while contributing to the growth of the sugar industry.

Our Values

  • Quality: Uncompromising commitment to excellence in every project
  • Innovation: Continuous improvement and adoption of latest technologies
  • Integrity: Honest and transparent business practices
  • Customer Focus: Building long-term relationships through superior service

Our Mission

To deliver efficient, economical, and sustainable sugar plant solutions that ensure long equipment life, dependable operation, and maximum value for our customers. We are committed to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction in every project we undertake.

Through our expertise and dedication, we aim to empower sugar mills with solutions that enhance productivity, reduce operational costs, and promote sustainable manufacturing practices.
