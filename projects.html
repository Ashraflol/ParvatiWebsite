Our Project Execution Approach

Systematic methodology ensuring quality and timely delivery

Phase 1: Consultation & Planning

Initial consultation to understand requirements, site assessment, feasibility studies, and detailed project planning with timeline and milestones.

Phase 2: Design & Engineering

Detailed process engineering, equipment design, civil and structural engineering drawings, electrical and instrumentation design, and approval of all drawings.

Phase 3: Manufacturing & Procurement

Manufacturing of equipment as per specifications, quality control at every stage, procurement of materials and components, and pre-dispatch inspection.

Phase 4: Erection & Installation

Transportation to site, systematic erection as per layout, installation of all equipment and systems, and integration of electrical and instrumentation systems.

Phase 5: Commissioning & Handover

Trial runs and testing, performance verification, operator training, documentation handover, and post-commissioning support.
